## Getting Started

### Create a Game
1. Click **CREATE GAME** on the main menu
2. You're now the host — share the 5-character room code with friends (click [COPY])
3. Pick a color and get your random bot name (♻ to reroll)
4. Click **START GAME** when everyone's in

### Join a Game
1. Click **JOIN GAME** on the main menu
2. Type the 5-character room code (or click [PASTE])
3. Click **ENTER LOBBY**
4. Pick a color and wait for the host to start

## The Scrapyard

<img src="../images/bunnybot.png" alt="Bunnybot" width="25%"/><br />
You control a factory machine (🏭) in a shared scrapyard. The grid is made of tiles — your goal is to own the most tiles when time runs out.

## Controls

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Claim a tile | Click any neutral tile next to your territory |
| Mine a gear | Click a ⚙ tile that's unclaimed or yours |
| Upgrade Attack | Click the ⚔ ATK button |
| Upgrade Defense | Click the 🛡 DEF button |
| Steer growth | Press arrow keys (↑↓←→) |
| Clear direction | Press the same arrow again, or Escape |

## How to Win

- **Expand** — Click neutral tiles next to your territory to claim them. Each tile costs scrap, and the cost goes up as you grow.
- **Earn scrap** — You earn 1 scrap per tile you own, every second. More tiles = more income.
- **Mine gears** — ⚙ tiles have 50 scrap each. Click them to extract scrap equal to your attack stat.
- **Upgrade** — Spend scrap on attack (to push borders and mine faster) or defense (to hold your borders).
- **Absorb opponents** — When border conflict takes all of an opponent's tiles, they join your team.

## Border Conflict

Borders between players are resolved automatically every second:
- Your **attack × border tiles** is your pressure
- Their **defense × border tiles** is their resistance
- If your pressure beats their resistance, you take one of their border tiles
- If it's equal, nothing happens (stalemate)

## Getting Absorbed

If you lose all your tiles, you join the team that absorbed you:
- Your adjective gets added to the team name
- You can still click to claim tiles and mine gears for your team
- Only the team lead can spend scrap on upgrades
- Your screen still shows your team in your original color

## Tips

- Early game: expand fast to build income
- Mid game: upgrade attack to mine gears and push borders
- Don't neglect defense — one strong neighbor can eat you quickly
- Use arrow keys to focus your expansion toward gears or weak opponents
- After absorption, help your team by clicking gears and claiming tiles
